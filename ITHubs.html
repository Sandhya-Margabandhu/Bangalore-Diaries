A GROWING CORPORATE HUB OF INDIA


Bangalore is the Silicon Valley of India and the city of dreams where once you come it’s hard to leave. This city is famously known for its amazingly carved Tech Parks and these tech parks host hundreds of top-notch IT companies.Bangalore is one of the most-preferred cities to live in with top-notch social infrastructure, picture-perfect roadways, impeccable educational institutes and all the components needed for a thriving lifestyle.


TOP 3 IT TECH PARKS IN BANGALORE
Electronic City Tech Park

Electronic city is one of India’s largest Electronic IT industrial parks spread over 800 acres in Konappana Agrahara and Doddathogur villages in Bangalore. It is divided into four phases and is established by Keonics, Karnataka Electronic, a pioneer in IT infrastructure development. This infrastructure is a home for 200 IT companies which include main campuses of IT/Biotech majors like BHEL, Infosys, Wipro, TCS, HCL, Tech Mahindra, C-Dot, etc.

Bagmane Tech Park

With its magnificent infrastructure, Bagmane IT park is easily identifiable, located at CV Raman Nagar in Bangalore sharing boundaries with HAL and DRDO. Bagmane tech park is suited for people living in Indira Nagar, Jeevan Bheema Nagar and Sadanand Nagar. It’s one of the parks with a lake near, which is frequently visited by employees of the companies on a break.

Manyata Embassy Business Park

Spreading over 300 acres, Manyata Tech Park is a software technology park in Bangalore, Karnataka, awarded as Best IT Park by NDTC property awards. This tech park is close to the residential area like Hennur, Hebbal, Banaswad and connected with the Outer Ring Road area as Whitefield. This property renders a gardening area and a rooftop football arena as well.
